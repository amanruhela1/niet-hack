Why Proper Disposal of E-Waste is Important

Improper disposal of e-waste can lead to serious environmental and health problems. E-waste often contains toxic materials such as lead, mercury, and cadmium that can pollute soil and water, and cause harm to humans and animals. By properly disposing of e-waste, we can protect the environment and ensure the health and safety of our communities.
